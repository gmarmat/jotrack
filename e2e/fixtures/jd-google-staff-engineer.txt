Staff Software Engineer - Infrastructure

Mountain View, CA | Sunnyvale, CA

• 5 years of experience with data structures or algorithms
• 3 years of experience with full stack development, across back-end (e.g., Java, Python, GO, or C++) and front-end (e.g., JavaScript or TypeScript, HTML, CSS, or equivalent)

Preferred Qualifications:
• Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science or related technical field

• Experience with Google Cloud Platform or similar cloud technologies

About the Job:
Google's software engineers develop the next-generation technologies that change how billions of users connect, explore, and interact with information and one another. Our products need to handle information at massive scale, and extend well beyond web search. We're looking for engineers who bring fresh ideas from all areas, including information retrieval, distributed computing, large-scale system design, networking and data storage, security, artificial intelligence, natural language processing, UI design and mobile.

As a Staff Software Engineer on the Infrastructure team, you will work on critical projects that power Google's core services. You'll design, develop, test, deploy, maintain, and enhance large-scale software solutions. You'll manage project priorities, deadlines, and deliverables while applying your expertise to lead and influence technical direction.

Responsibilities:
• Design and implement large-scale distributed systems that handle billions of requests per day
• Lead technical design discussions and code reviews, setting best practices for team of 20+ engineers
• Influence technical strategy and architecture decisions across multiple teams
• Mentor and develop engineers, fostering technical growth and career development
• Drive adoption of new technologies and methodologies to improve system reliability and performance
• Collaborate with Product Managers, UX designers, and other engineers to deliver user-facing features
• Participate in on-call rotation, ensuring 99.99% uptime for critical services
• Contribute to engineering culture through tech talks, design reviews, and documentation

Technical Skills Expected:
• Expert-level proficiency in at least two programming languages (Python, Go, Java, C++)
• Deep understanding of distributed systems, scalability, and performance optimization
• Experience with large-scale data processing (MapReduce, Apache Beam, Dataflow)
• Strong knowledge of computer science fundamentals (algorithms, data structures, system design)
• Experience with Google Cloud Platform, Kubernetes, Docker
• Familiarity with monitoring, logging, and observability (Stackdriver, Prometheus, Grafana)
• Database expertise (Bigtable, Spanner, PostgreSQL, MySQL)
• Understanding of machine learning and AI (preferred)

Leadership & Soft Skills:
• Proven ability to lead technical projects from conception to launch
• Strong communication skills (written and verbal) for technical and non-technical audiences

• Demonstrated ability to mentor and develop engineers
